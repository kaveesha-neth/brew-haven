Trending drinks

Cappuccino

A timeless classic that balances bold espresso with creamy steamed milk and a frothy layer of foam. The cappuccino offers a rich coffee flavor with a light, airy texture, perfect for those who enjoy a well-rounded and satisfying coffee experience.

Price : $3.75

Latte

A creamy, smooth espresso-based drink made with steamed milk and just a touch of foam on top. The latte’s mild flavor and velvety texture make it a favorite for coffee lovers of all kinds. Customize it with your choice of syrups like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut for a personalized twist.

Price : $4.00

Americano

For those who appreciate the pure essence of espresso, the Americano is a simple yet flavorful drink made by diluting espresso with hot water. It’s smooth, mellow, and offers a lighter version of traditional espresso, making it ideal for any time of the day.

Price : $3.00

Mocha

Indulge in the sweet and satisfying combination of rich espresso, smooth steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with a delicate layer of foam. The mocha is the perfect choice for those who crave both the deep flavors of coffee and the sweet comfort of chocolate.

Price : $4.50

Iced Latte

A refreshing twist on the classic latte, served cold with chilled milk and espresso over ice. The iced latte is perfect for a cool, creamy treat on a warm day, offering the same smooth and balanced flavor of a traditional latte with a refreshing chill.

Price : $4.25

Cold Brew

Slow-steeped for 12 hours to bring out the full, smooth flavor with minimal acidity, cold brew is the perfect drink for those who enjoy a mellow, rich coffee flavor. Its smoothness and naturally sweet taste make it a favorite among coffee aficionados looking for a refreshing cold drink.

Price : $4.50
